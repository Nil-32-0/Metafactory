#Mod/AE2 #RecipeType/Machine

<ins>Recipes</ins>
Charges items with power
Inputs: 
- 1 Item
- Power (Fixed amount)
Outputs:
- 1+ Item

No built-in method, use custom recipe constructor from `AE2Recipes` instead.
Recipe Syntax:
```
AE2Recipes.charger(output, input)
```

Parameter Types:
All are `ItemStack`
